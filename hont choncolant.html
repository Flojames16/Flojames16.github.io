You will need:

  • 3 x heaped spoons of your favourite Whittard hot chocolate
  • 250ml x milk
  • 1 x mug
    • Optional:
  • Marshmallows
  • Whipped cream

Method:

  1. Add three heaped teaspoons of hot chocolate powder to your mug
  2. Add 250ml of milk
  3. Stir until smooth
  4. Put in microwavc for 1 minute
  5. Take out and stir until smooth
  6. Place back into microwave for another minute
  7. Take out and allow to cool
    8. Optional:
  8. Add whipped cream
  9. Add marshmallows

    10. Enjoy!
